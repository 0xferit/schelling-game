    Rules

    Round Flow

    Each round has three phases: Commit (60s), Reveal (15s), Results (20s). Every attached player antes 60 per round.

    Who Wins

    Players on the exact-match plurality option win. If multiple options tie for the highest count, all tied groups win.

    Payout

    The pot is playerCount × 60. Winners split it equally; losers forfeit their ante.

    Voided Rounds

    If nobody reveals in time, the round is voided and all antes are refunded.

    Solo Revealer

    If only one player reveals, they win the entire pot but earn no coordination credit.

    Coordination Credit

    Awarded only when a winner's option was picked by at least 2 players. Solo wins and all-distinct rounds give zero coordination credit.

    Disconnect / Forfeit

    A disconnected player has 15 seconds to reconnect. After that they forfeit but remain attached for pot accounting: they lose their ante each remaining round.

    Examples

    2-1 split (3 players, pot = 180): Two players pick the same option. Each winner receives 90 (net +30). The lone picker gets nothing (net −60).
    Solo reveal (3 players, 1 valid reveal, pot = 180): That player wins the full 180 (net +120) but earns no coordination credit because only one option was revealed.
