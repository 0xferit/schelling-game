    Rules

    Game Flow

    Each game flows through Commit (60s), auto-reveal, and Settle (7s). Every attached player antes 2520 per game.

    Who Wins

    Players on the exact-match plurality option win. If multiple options tie for the highest count, all tied groups win.

    Payout

    The pot is playerCount × 2520. Winners split it equally; losers forfeit their ante. Any indivisible remainder is burned.

    Voided Games

    If nobody reveals in time, the game is voided and all antes are refunded.

    Solo Revealer

    If only one player reveals, they win the entire pot but earn no coordination credit.

    Coordination Credit

    Awarded only when a winner's option was picked by at least 2 players. Solo wins and all-distinct games give zero coordination credit.

    Disconnect / Forfeit

    A disconnected player has 15 seconds to reconnect. After that they forfeit: all remaining antes are charged as a one-time penalty and they are removed from all subsequent games.

    AI Backfill

    If a bot backfills a public match, the entire match is off the record. You still play the full game, but balances, streaks, and leaderboard standing do not change.

    Examples

    2-1 split (3 players, pot = 7560): Two players pick the same option. Each winner receives 3780 (net +1260). The lone picker gets nothing (net −2520).
    Solo reveal (3 players, 1 valid reveal, pot = 7560): That player wins the full 7560 (net +5040) but earns no coordination credit because only one option was revealed.
